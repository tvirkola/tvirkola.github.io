About

I'm an Academy Research Fellow at VATT Institute for Economic Research and an affiliate at the Helsinki GSE, RFBerlin and FIT. I defended my PhD at the European University Institute in 2021.

My work focuses on applied labor economics, migration, public economics, education and economic history.

I'm visiting at Universitat Pompeu Fabra in the Fall 2025.
